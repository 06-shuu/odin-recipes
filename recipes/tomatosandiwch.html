Tomato Sandwich

Description:

The Tomato Mayo Sandwich is a tasty and easy-to-enjoy treat. It's a simple combination of deliciousness tucked between two slices of toasted bread. Whether for a quick bite or a light meal, this sandwich brings straightforward satisfaction with its straightforward flavors and textures.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Mayonnaise
  • Bread

Steps:

  1. Cut the tomatoes into thick slices and sprinkle them with black pepper and salt.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and toast the bread until it turns golden brown.
  3. Spread mayonnaise on the bread and add two slices of tomatoes.
  4. Done!
